# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Uzox ("Uzox", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you add, access, or use the Uzox Discord application, website, dashboard, support channels, premium features, or related services (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Service and remove the application from any Discord server you manage.

## Information We Collect

We try to collect only what is reasonably needed to operate, protect, and improve the Service. Depending on how you use the Service, this may include:

- Discord account, server, channel, role, interaction, and permission identifiers.
- Commands, settings, preferences, queues, saved items, history, and other content or metadata needed to provide requested features.
- Basic usage, diagnostics, device, log, security, and error information.
- Support messages, reports, feedback, and related contact information you choose to provide.
- Subscription, entitlement, transaction status, and billing-related metadata when needed to provide paid features. Payment details are handled by the applicable payment provider.
- Information from third-party integrations only when you choose to connect or use them, and only as needed to provide the requested feature.

We may process information automatically through Discord, our hosting providers, databases, analytics, security tools, payment providers, support tools, and other service providers that help us operate the Service.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide, personalize, maintain, and improve the Service.
- Store and apply server settings, user settings, queues, premium status, and feature preferences.
- Respond to support requests, reports, abuse, security issues, and legal requests.
- Prevent misuse, enforce our Terms, protect users, and protect the Service.
- Debug, monitor, measure reliability, and develop new or improved features.
- Comply with applicable law, platform rules, and third-party requirements.

We do not sell Discord API data. We do not use Discord API data for advertising networks or data brokers. We do not use message content obtained through Discord APIs to train artificial intelligence models unless Discord expressly allows it and applicable law permits it.

## Sharing Information

We may share information:

- With service providers that help operate, secure, host, analyze, support, or process payments for the Service.
- With Discord, platform providers, payment providers, rights holders, or other third parties when needed to operate features, investigate abuse, enforce terms, handle reports, or comply with applicable rules.
- If required by law, court order, legal process, or a good-faith safety, security, or rights-protection need.
- In connection with a merger, transfer, reorganization, or similar change involving the Service.
- In aggregated or de-identified form that does not reasonably identify you.

## Retention and Deletion

We keep information only for as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required for security, backups, dispute resolution, legal compliance, abuse prevention, or enforcement.

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us through the official Uzox support server. We may need enough information to verify the request. Some information may remain where retention is required or permitted by law, platform rules, backups, security, or legitimate operational needs.

If the Service is removed from a server, related server settings and operational data may be deleted or retained for a limited period for backup, abuse prevention, audit, or restoration purposes.
